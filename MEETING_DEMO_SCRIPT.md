# Sunshine Rewards Meeting Demo Script

Use this script for a 5-7 minute executive walkthrough.

## Opening Position

Sunshine Rewards is being shown as an executive prototype, not a production launch. The goal is to demonstrate what a unified digital rewards experience could look like across Sunshine Finance and Sunshine Insurance, and how the internal team could manage customer engagement, redemptions, and executive visibility.

Recommended opening:

> Today we want to show the loyalty concept from three views: the customer experience, the admin/staff experience, and the executive reporting view. The prototype is intentionally focused on Sunshine Finance and Sunshine Insurance based on the current scope direction.

## 1. Customer Experience

Start from the landing screen.

Say:

> A customer starts with one Sunshine Rewards account. Instead of managing rewards manually or through disconnected processes, they can see their points, linked Sunshine companies, offers, card, and activity in one place.

Steps:

1. Click **Start Customer Demo**.
2. Sign in as Alicia Rolle.
3. Show the home screen.
4. Point out points balance, tier, quick actions, linked companies, and recent activity.
5. Open **Card**.

Say:

> The card gives the customer a simple member ID and scan-style experience for staff verification. In production, this would use a real QR code connected to the customer's profile.

6. Open **Offers**.
7. Redeem an available reward.
8. Show the success code.

Say:

> The redemption code is important because it creates a controlled bridge between customer self-service and staff verification.

## 2. Admin / Staff Experience

Open the Admin Portal.

Say:

> The admin portal gives staff and managers visibility into the program. This is where they can review customers, manage campaigns, validate redemptions, and keep an audit trail.

Steps:

1. Show the desktop admin dashboard.
2. Point out the left navigation and KPI area.
3. Point out the **Redemption Queue**.
4. Click **Verify Codes**.
5. Search or locate the code issued from the customer flow.
6. Click **Mark Used**.

Say:

> This avoids the customer redeeming a reward digitally while staff still have to manually guess or reconcile the activity later. The system creates a visible status: issued, used, voided, or expired.

7. Open **Customers**.
8. Select Alicia Rolle.
9. Show linked Finance and Insurance accounts.
10. Use **Adjust Points** with a reason.
11. Show **Add Note** and **Send Offer** as demo admin actions.

Say:

> In production, these actions would be permission-controlled and written to a secure audit trail.

## 3. Executive View

Open Executive Dashboard.

Say:

> The executive dashboard is designed to answer whether the loyalty program is driving engagement, redemption, and cross-company participation.

Steps:

1. Show top KPIs.
2. Show program growth.
3. Show redemption operations insight.
4. Show company performance for Sunshine Finance and Sunshine Insurance.

Say:

> This is where leadership can see the program as more than a points feature. It becomes a customer engagement and data visibility layer across the group.

## Key Value Proposition

Recommended language:

> The value is not simply giving points. The value is creating a unified customer account, digital redemption process, auditable staff workflow, and better data visibility across selected Sunshine companies.

## What We Need From Sunshine Next

Ask for:

1. Confirm final pilot companies.
2. Confirm what customer actions should earn points.
3. Confirm what rewards are commercially acceptable.
4. Identify system owners for Finance and Insurance data.
5. Decide whether the next phase is a functional Supabase MVP or a more polished clickable prototype.

## Suggested Next Phase

Recommended positioning:

> The next step should be a functional MVP using Supabase, secure point operations, customer profiles, linked accounts, rewards, redemptions, admin roles, and a simple reporting layer. We would keep integrations controlled until Sunshine confirms the data sources and business rules.

## Presenter Reminder

Keep the walkthrough focused on Sunshine Finance and Sunshine Insurance unless Sunshine explicitly changes the scope direction.
